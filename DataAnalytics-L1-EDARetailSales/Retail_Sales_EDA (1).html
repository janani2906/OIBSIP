Conclusion¶

From the exploratory data analysis of the retail sales dataset, the following insights were observed:

  1. Sales fluctuate across different months and quarters, indicating seasonal purchasing patterns.

  2. The customer base consists of both male and female customers, with a balanced distribution.

  3. Customers from certain age groups contribute more to overall sales.

  4. Product categories generate different levels of revenue, helping identify high-performing categories.

  5. Quantity and price per unit have a strong influence on the total purchase amount.

Business Recommendations¶

• Increase stock for high-performing product categories.

• Introduce promotional offers during low-sales months.

• Target marketing campaigns towards the most active customer age groups.

• Monitor pricing strategies to maximize revenue.

• Use customer purchasing patterns for personalized marketing.
